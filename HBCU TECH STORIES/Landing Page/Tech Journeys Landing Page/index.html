HBCU Tech Journeys

An individual life story has the power to inspire and motivate,
and shine as the light of hope that drives others to dream more, learn more, do more and become more for themselves and their community.
Every journey we share is a light in the darkness that seeks to inspire and to enlighten every student!

What We Do

We believe that an individual story has the power to change the lives of many people around the world - it has the power to inspire and motivate, and instill great hope! Our goal is to bring together the unique and diverse life journeys of individuals in tech from HBCU Colleges and Universities - people who have overcome great obstacles to be where they are now. Our vision is that through these stories, students, especially those from HBCUs will be inspired to pursue their dreams in the tech industry. If you have any question, you can contact us or chat with us on our Email.

Journey Maps

Journey maps give a way for individuals to visualize their life journeys in our platform. The life journeys can be as big as one's life - from the day they were born to the point where they are now - and as small as one's day. The idea is that, people get to visualize their life and see how they have progressd from one point to the next, and learn from those experiences.

We Support and Guide You!

Whatever concerns or challenges you may have, our team is there to support you. Our goal is to make sure that individuals who use our platform have the best resources that they need to make their tech journeys a success.

Connections

Security and privacy of individuals in our community is a top priority. We strive to make each individual experience personal and specific to the individual in question. Each individual has the power to choose their connections, community, mentors, and friends in the platform. Also, it is up to the individual to make their journey map(s) visible and availbale to the whole community.
